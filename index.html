Welcome to Xiomy's Cake Creations!

At Xiomy's we strive to provide our customer's with the cake of their dreams. Each of our cakes is unique and created with special care based on our customer's request. Whatever your budget, our lead pastry chef, Xiomara, will work with you personally to provide you the cake you envision. Follow the steps below to get started.

Let's get started!

Step 1: Choose a theme and color combination that suits you. If you're searching for some inspiration, take a look at our gallery.

Step 2: Determine how many guests the cake will be served. We can create cakes that serve 25-150+ guests.

Step 3: Decide on a shape for the cake. We have round, square, and combinations of the shapes. We also specialize in custom shapes.

Step 4: Establish a budget for your cake creation.

Step 5: Details about your event such as location, date, time, and type of event.


We are excited to be a part of your memorable event. Give us a call at (516) 395-1355 to further discuss how we can bring the cake of your dreams to life!
